All About Sweet Potatoes

The best vegetable there is

The sweet potatoes is starchy, large root vegetable with sweet taste. They are known to contain relatively high nutrional value, most notably the vitamin beta-carotene. They can be baked, steamed, and fried.

Top Producers

  1. China
  2. Nigeria
  3. Uganda

Fun Facts

  • Sweet Pototaes are only distantly related to potatoes
  • Skin of sweet potatoes can vary in color such as purple, white, and pink
